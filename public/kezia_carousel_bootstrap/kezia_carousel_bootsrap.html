Wonders of the World


Hello Wanderers! Did you know? In 2001, an initiative was started by the Swiss corporation New7Wonders Foundation to choose the New7Wonders of the World from a selection of 200 existing monuments through online votes. The Great Pyramid of Giza, the only remaining wonder of the traditional Seven Wonders of the Ancient World, was not one of the winners announced in 2007 but was added as an honorary candidate.

The New7Wonders of the World includes the Taj Mahal, Colosseum, Chichen Itza, Machu Picchu, Christ The Redeemer, Great Wall of China, Petra, and obviously Pyramids of Giza. TIPS BEFORE YOU VISIT THE WALL

  • Wear comfortable hiking or walking shoes and be prepared for stairs and steep terrain.
  • Start as early as you can to avoid the crowds and enjoy the serenity of the Wall.
  • Food stalls and restaurants are not always available. Bring snacks to munch on.
  • Check the frequency of public transportation (buses and trains) in advance, or pre-book a cab.
  • Limit your water intake as there is no washroom beyond the main entrance.

TIPS FOR VISITING THE PYRAMIDS OF GIZA

  • Get there before 10 AM.
  • Wear good walking shoes.
